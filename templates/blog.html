Building a Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App with Flask and Machine Learning

The Problem

Many individuals experience symptoms and seek immediate clarity about potential health issues. Given the overlap of symptoms across various ailments, pinpointing a specific disease can be challenging. Our objective was to create a user-friendly tool that offers potential diagnoses based on the symptoms provided.

The Solution

Users input their symptoms into the application, which then processes this data through a pre-trained Decision Tree Classifier. The system analyzes the input to predict the most probable disease. Beyond the diagnosis, the app offers detailed information about the identified condition, including descriptions, recommended precautions, medication options, dietary suggestions, and exercise recommendations.

Key Features

  • User-Friendly Interface: Designed for ease of use, allowing users to seamlessly input symptoms.
  • Reliable Predictions: The machine learning model delivers accurate disease forecasts based on user data.
  • Comprehensive Health Information: Provides in-depth details about potential conditions and actionable health advice.
  • Educational Resources: Features informative blog posts on various health topics to enhance user knowledge.

Conclusion

Our symptom-based disease diagnosis web application merges technology with healthcare, offering users immediate insights into potential health concerns. By integrating machine learning with a user-centric design, we aim to make healthcare information more accessible and personalized. This initiative strives to empower individuals with knowledge, promoting proactive health management and contributing to overall well-being.
